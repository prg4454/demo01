AI Development Budget

This project works with an AI budget for development so AI assistance stays useful, predictable, and intentionally reviewed.

The budget is a planned allowance for AI-supported work such as drafting code, suggesting refactors, generating sample content, and helping inspect defects. It is not treated as unlimited automation. The team decides where AI adds leverage and where direct human implementation or review is still required.

How It Works

  • Work is scoped first so AI is used on clear, bounded tasks instead of open-ended churn.
  • AI output is reviewed by a developer before it is accepted into the application.
  • Time, prompts, and tool usage are treated as part of the delivery cost for the feature.
  • Higher-risk changes still require stronger validation, testing, and human judgment.

In practice, that means AI can accelerate iteration, but the budget keeps the project from spending too much effort on low-value experiments. The goal is better throughput with clear guardrails around quality, review, and maintainability.
